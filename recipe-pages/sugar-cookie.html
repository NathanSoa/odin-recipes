Sugar Cookies

Description

sugar cookies that are crisp on the outside and very chewy on the inside. Do not try to roll these cookies out, they are meant to be shaped or dropped. This recipe can easily be made into snickerdoodle cookies by rolling the dough in cinnamon-sugar before baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups margarine
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanille extract
  • ¼ cup white sugar for decoration
